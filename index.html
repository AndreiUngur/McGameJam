What is McGame Jam?

McGame Jam is a very recent initiative to develop McGill's game developer community. Founded by Gerald Lang in 2015, it is a 48 hour competition in which teams composed of people from different backgrounds come together in order to create a game based on a give theme.

McGame Jam welcomes all students and is a great opportunity for people with little experience to try out game development. Don't know how to code? You can do gameplay and visual design or even better, learn how to code! Already know how to code but don't know where to start? That's ok, we have a team of mentors who will be available to help out.

McGame Jam will be held on the weekend of X at École de Technologie Supérieure (ÉTS)

Preparation

In order to enjoy this competition to the fullest, we recommend you get familiar with these programming languages as well as tools:

  • Python+Pygame
  • Javascript
  • C#
  • C++
  • Unity
  • Unreal Engine
  • Mixamo
